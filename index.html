What is VidKraftr?

VidKraftr is a script-first AI video generator that turns your ideas into viral faceless videos. Unlike other text-to-video tools, we start by generating an AI-optimized script with hooks and emotional triggers, then automatically build the video around it with matched stock footage, text overlays, and transitions.

How do I create a faceless video?

Describe your idea, pick your platform (TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels), choose an objective (engagement, growth, sales), and click generate. AI writes the script, matches HD stock footage, and renders a finished video in under 2 minutes.

Do I need video editing experience?

Not at all. VidKraftr is fully automated — the AI handles scriptwriting, footage selection, and video assembly. You just describe what you want. No camera, no editing software, no technical skills required.

Which platforms can I publish to?

VidKraftr generates vertical short-form videos (9:16) optimized for TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and Facebook Reels. Scripts and pacing are tailored to each platform's algorithm and audience behavior.
