What is VidKraftr and how does it create faceless videos?

How do I make a faceless video with AI?

Three steps: (1) Describe your idea or paste a script, (2) Choose your target platform and content objective (engagement, growth, sales), (3) Click generate. VidKraftr’s AI writes the script, finds matching stock footage from Pexels, and renders the final video automatically. Most videos are ready in under 2 minutes.

What platforms and video formats does VidKraftr support?

VidKraftr generates vertical short-form videos (9:16) optimized for TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and Facebook Reels. What is Inspiration Mode and how does it help me go viral?

Inspiration Mode lets you paste the URL of any viral video (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram). You can then generate a new script that applies those same viral patterns to your own niche.
